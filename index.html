Glad You Are Still There

Ongoing Collection of Nature Webcams

While being sheltered in place, I wonder what it is like being out there, in nature. Are they still there when I am not there? I started to watch a lot of webcams live streaming animals on Youtube, like hummingbirds in Studio City, California, and wildlifes in Mozambique. I watch them transit from dawn to dusk, in their different timezones.

Each live video has a number at the bottom left showing how many people are watching it right now. The viewing number, along with the feeling that I might not be alone, gives me comfort. Another notable moment is when the number drops to one. The second when there is nobody but just me, the bird songs, the trees, the breeze, and the updated frames, is the moment I feel grateful that they are still there, together.
